# THANNIGO - Water Delivery App Design Brief ## 🎨 COLOR SYSTEM - **Primary Blue:** #0077BE (CTAs, active nav, primary buttons, focus) - **Secondary Teal:** #48BCAE (Success, verified, delivered) - **Tertiary Mint:** #A0DEBF (Tags, soft highlights, accents) - **Neutral Grey:** #74777C (Secondary text, dividers, placeholders) - **Background:** #F4F6FA (App background, card fills) - **Dark Navy:** #1A2340 (Headings, primary body text, icons) - **Alert Red:** #FF5252 (Errors, cancel, rejected) - **Warning Amber:** #FF9800 (Pending, low stock, COD badges) - **White:** #FFFFFF (Card surfaces, active button text) - **Light Blue Bg:** #EEF7FF (Chip backgrounds, selected highlights) - **Mint Bg:** #E8F7F4 (Success banners) - **Amber Bg:** #FFF3E0 (Warning cards) ## 🏗️ COMPONENT STYLES - **Buttons:** - Primary: bg #0077BE, white text, h 52px, radius 14px - Secondary: bg white, border 1.5px #0077BE, #0077BE text - **Cards:** White bg, border #DDE6F5, radius 14px, shadow 0 2px 12px rgba(0,119,190,0.08) - **Typography:** Arial. Heading 20px bold #1A2340, Body 14px #74777C, Price 24-40px bold #0077BE ## 📱 SCREEN PLAN SUMMARY - **Phase 1:** Auth & Onboarding (Splash, Onboarding Carousel, Phone Login + OTP) - **Phase 2:** Customer Discovery (Dashboard, Search/Filter/Map, Shop Profile, Availability) - **Phase 3:** Order Placement & Payment (Checkout, Confirmation, Schedule, Cancel, Wallet, Delivery OTP) - **Phase 4:** Order Tracking & Profile (Live Tracking, History, Profile/Settings, Rate & Review) - **Phase 5:** Shop Operations (Dashboard, Order Detail, Inventory, Delivery Management, Customer Management, Busy Mode) - **Phase 6:** Earnings & Analytics (Earnings, Analytics, Notifications/Complaints, Shop Settings, Subscriptions, SOS/Help) - **Phase 7:** Extended Features (Vendor Registration, Customer Subscriptions, Report Issue, Edit Order, Delivery App Flow, Manual Order, Stock-In, Credit Tracking)
